About M.Shawki

Captain Mohamed Shawki is a seasoned professional in the fitness and sports coaching industry with over two decades of experience. He serves as the Owner and General Manager of Titanium Gym in Mansoura, Egypt, and the TASS Academy for Sports Sciences. In addition to his coaching roles, he lectures at the National Guard Health Affairs, King Abdul Aziz Medical City in Riyadh, KSA. Captain Shawki has achieved significant success in bodybuilding and taekwondo competitions, highlighting his expertise and dedication to physical fitness. He continuously seeks to enhance his knowledge through various certifications and diplomas, showcasing his commitment to staying updated with the latest trends in sports science and training methodologies. Overall, Captain Mohamed Shawki is a passionate and dedicated individual who leaves a lasting impact on those he coaches and mentors
